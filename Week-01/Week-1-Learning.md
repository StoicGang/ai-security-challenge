# Week 1 Learning — AI Foundations

## What I Set Out to Understand
How does a neural network learn? From raw numbers to tokens to embeddings to semantic search.

## The Four Core Concepts
1. **Gradient Descent** — moving downhill in loss space
2. **Neural Networks** — stacking weighted layers + activation functions
3. **Tokenization** — breaking text into trainable units
4. **Embeddings** — converting discrete tokens into continuous vectors

## The Journey
- Days 1–2: Understood how individual components (loss, gradients, neurons) work
- Day 3: Built a tokenizer — first time "building" something from scratch rather than using a library
- Day 4: Embeddings clicked when I realized they're just vectors in high-dimensional space where similar meanings are spatially close
- Day 5: Semantic search was the payoff — seeing it retrieve relevant articles proved the entire chain worked
